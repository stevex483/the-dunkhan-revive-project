# 1. Overview

Kraaftlott was one of the original Minecraft roleplay series produced by Dunkhan & SuperMonkeyMan (TreizsoftGames).

The series was a Minecraft parody of the French television series Kaamelott, recreating its universe, characters and humor within the game.

# 3. Description

Kraaftlott was an original Minecraft roleplay comedy series created by Dunkhan & SuperMonkeyMan.

Inspired by Kaamelott, the series recreated many of its iconic characters, locations and comedic style within Minecraft while adapting them to its own original storyline.

Dunkhan portrayed King Dunkhan, a parody of King Arthur, while SuperMonkeyMan portrayed Seigneur MonkeyMan.

Several other French Minecraft content creators also participated in the series, portraying characters inspired by Kaamelott or original adaptations created for Kraaftlott.

Only part of the original series has currently been recovered.

---

# 4. Recovery Status

🟡 **Partially Recovered**

Several episodes have been preserved through community reuploads.

Most of the original series remains lost following the deletion of the TreizsoftGames YouTube channel.
