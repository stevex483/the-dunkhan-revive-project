# 3. Description

HunterZ was a Minecraft survival roleplay series set in a zombie apocalypse, originally created by Gussdx.

The series featured multiple French Minecraft content creators playing together as survivors in a shared world.

HunterZ was played in Hardcore mode: each participant had only one life. Once a survivor died, their adventure ended permanently, making every decision and encounter highly consequential.

The TreizsoftGames channel published Dunkhan's point of view. Although the videos were recorded from Dunkhan's perspective, both Dunkhan and SuperMonkeyMan were active participants throughout the adventure alongside Gussdx and the other survivors.

HunterZ became one of the most influential Minecraft roleplay series in the French YouTube community. Its success inspired numerous later series and community projects based on the same concept.

# 4. Recovery Status

🟡 **Partially Recovered**

Several episodes have been preserved through community reuploads.

However, a significant portion of the original series remains lost following the deletion of the TreizsoftGames YouTube channel.
