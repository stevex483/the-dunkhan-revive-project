# 3. Description

Planète Minecraft was an original Minecraft roleplay adventure series created by Dunkhan & SuperMonkeyMan.

The story followed Dunkhan as he travelled across unusual worlds and dimensions in search of mysterious artifacts assigned by Jacques Tartino.

The series featured several recurring fictional characters, including Jacques Tartino, the fictional producer of the adventure portrayed and voiced by Dunkhan, and Le Mage, another recurring character.

One of the series' recognizable elements was Dunkhan's use of a fake TARDIS inspired by Doctor Who as a means of transportation between adventures.

Several episodes were inspired by popular culture. For example, the first episode, "Don't Blink", featured Weeping Angels inspired by Doctor Who.

Planète Minecraft became one of the channel's most memorable original roleplay series.
