## Objective

Determine whether the Internet Archive's Wayback Machine contains archived, playable copies of videos from the former YouTube channel TreizsoftGames (Dunkhan & SuperMonkeyMan).

---

## Background

The YouTube channel TreizsoftGames, better known as Dunkhan & SuperMonkeyMan, was deleted around 2015.

As a result, a large number of its videos are currently unavailable and are considered lost by the community.

The Wayback Machine was investigated as a potential source for recovering archived versions of these videos.

---

## Method

The following resources were examined:

- Original YouTube video URLs
- Original channel URL
- Archived YouTube watch pages
- Archived channel pages

The objective was to determine whether archived pages also preserved playable video streams.

---

## Findings

During this investigation:

- Archived snapshots of some YouTube pages were found.
- Some metadata (titles, page structure and related information) may still be preserved.
- No archived playable video streams were identified during this investigation.
- No recoverable video files were obtained from the examined snapshots.

---

## Conclusion

The Wayback Machine is a valuable source for recovering metadata and documenting deleted YouTube content.

However, based on the results of this investigation, it should not be considered a primary source for recovering the original video files.

---

## Evidence

Examples examined during this investigation included:

- Deleted YouTube video URLs
- Archived channel pages

No playable video files were recovered during this investigation.
